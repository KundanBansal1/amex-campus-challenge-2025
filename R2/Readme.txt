# Round 2 | Product Track | AMEX Campus Challenge 2025

## **Objective**
* Round 2 focuses on analyzing **clickstream and behavioral data** of AMEX card members to extract actionable insights and answer the 6 specific questions outlined in the problem statement.  
* The aim is to understand customer behavior, optimize offer presentations, and recommend the most relevant offers.

## **Problem Statement**
Participants are provided with **digital footprint data** capturing how customers interact with AMEX marketing offers. Key tasks include:  

* **Validating user behavior patterns**  
  - Active users  
  - Offer preferences  
  - Discount utilization  

* **Assessing offer effectiveness**  
  - Discount value  
  - Spending segments  
  - Reward points  

* **Identifying top-performing offers and customer profiles**  

* **Calculating engagement and click probabilities** for marketing campaigns  

## **Data Provided**
* `amex_offers_data.parquet` – Contains customer interactions, offer views, clicks, spend, and reward data  
* `Data_dictionary.xlsx` – Metadata describing variables in the dataset  

## **Tasks / Questions**
* Validate behavioral statements and calculate unique populations  
* Analyze impact of **high vs low discount value per dollar offers** across categories  
* Segment customers by spending and evaluate **reward point conversions**  
* Identify **top 10 offers by conversion rate** and associated customer spend/conversion metrics  
* Identify **top 15 customer profiles** from email campaigns and their offer engagement  
* Compute probabilities of offer and email clicks using **recency-weighted metrics (EWMA)**  

## **Submission & Evaluation**
* Solutions are evaluated based on **accuracy and completeness** for all questions  
* Partial scores may be awarded for partial answers  
* Leaderboard ranking considers **submission timestamp**; plagiarism checks are performed
