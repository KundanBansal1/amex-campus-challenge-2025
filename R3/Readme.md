# Round 3 | Product Track | AMEX Campus Challenge 2025

## **Overview**
For **Round 3** of the AMEX Campus Challenge Product Track, I developed and presented a **Go-to-Live strategy** for launching **personalized marketing offers** on a credit card website.  

My solution focused on building a **seamless, scalable, and customer-centric personalization engine** designed to drive user engagement and maximize conversion rates. As a result of this submission, I was selected as one of the winners.

## **My Approach**
The presentation and deck I prepared covered the following key areas:

* **Product Architecture**  
  - Designed a high-level system outlining **data flow**, the personalization engine, and website integration  

* **Backend Logic**  
  - Defined how user behavior, internal data, and external sources are leveraged to generate and deliver **personalized offers**  

* **Team Structure**  
  - Identified critical teams (Product, Engineering, Data Science, Marketing, Compliance) and described the **collaboration model**  

* **Launch Plan**  
  - Created a realistic **Go-to-Live plan** covering development, testing, deployment, and post-launch monitoring  

* **Risk Mitigation**  
  - Highlighted potential risks such as **data privacy and personalization errors** and proposed effective mitigation strategies  

* **Success Metrics**  
  - Defined KPIs to measure rollout success, including **conversion rate** and **time-to-offer**  

## **Outcome**
* Deck consisted of **7 slides** and presentation duration was under **7 minutes**  
* Focused on a **customer-first, technically robust, and execution-ready strategy**  
* **Selected as a winner** based on combined Round 2 performance and Round 3 presentation
